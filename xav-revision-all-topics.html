Ecosystems

All living things working together in one place

💡 An ecosystem = ALL living things (plants, animals, decomposers) + the non-living environment (water, light, temperature)
⚔️ Competition
🌿
Plants compete for... ☀️ Light  💧 Water  🌱 Minerals  📐 Space
🦁
Animals compete for... 🍖 Food  ❤️ Mates  🏠 Territory
♻️ Decay & Decomposers
🌡️ Warm Heat speeds up microorganisms
💧 Wet Water needed for decay
💨 Oxygen Aerobic bacteria work fastest

Warm + Wet + Oxygen = FAST decay

🌍 Biodiversity
🌈
What is it? The variety of different living things in an area
📈
Why does it matter? More biodiversity = healthier, more stable ecosystem
🧠
Remember! Organisms in an ecosystem are INTERDEPENDENT — they all need each other to survive.

Variation

Why living things are different from each other

💡 Variation = differences between living things. These differences come from genes, environment, or BOTH.
🎭 Two Types of Variation

📈 Continuous

  • Can be ANY value in a range
  • Examples: height, weight, arm length
  • Shown on a LINE GRAPH
  • No gaps between values

📊 Discontinuous

  • Only certain separate values
  • Examples: blood group, gender
  • Shown on a BAR CHART
  • Clear gaps — no in-between!
🧬 Where Does Variation Come From?
🧬
Genetic variation From your genes — e.g. eye colour, tongue rolling, blood group
🌍
Environmental variation From your surroundings — e.g. scars, hair style, skin tone from sun
🤝
Most characteristics = BOTH genes AND environment e.g. height is influenced by your genes AND by diet/nutrition growing up
🧠
Remember! Continuous = line graph, any value. Discontinuous = bar chart, set categories only.

Adaptation & Evolution

How living things survive and change over time

💡 Better adapted organisms survive, reproduce, and pass on their helpful traits. Over millions of years — this is evolution!
🐾 Adaptation Examples
🐻‍❄️
Polar Bear White fur (camouflage) · Thick fat (insulation) · Small ears (less heat loss)
🌵
Cactus Spines (reduce water loss + protection) · Fleshy stem (stores water) · Waxy coat (stops evaporation)
🔬 Natural Selection (Darwin's Big Idea)
1 Variation exists in a population
2 Some have helpful traits
3 They survive better
4 They reproduce more
5 Traits passed to offspring
🦠 Evolution Evidence
🦕
Fossils Remains in rock showing how life has changed over millions of years
💊
Antibiotic-resistant bacteria Natural selection happening RIGHT NOW — resistant bacteria survive and reproduce
🧠
Remember! "Survival of the fittest" — fittest means BEST ADAPTED, not strongest. Over time, this leads to evolution.
